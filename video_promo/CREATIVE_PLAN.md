# LeapCreww Healthcare — "Instant Checkup" · Creative Brief

A high-taste, beat-synced 30-second promo showcasing how LeapCreww automates doctor appointment bookings and payments inside WhatsApp.

---

## 1. Story Arc (30s / 900f @ 30fps / 120 BPM)

Timing is structured with 15 frames per beat and 60 frames per bar (2 seconds per bar).

| Act | Title | Frames | Timing | Palette | Beat Role |
|-----|-------|--------|--------|---------|-----------|
| I | **The Friction** | 0–150 | 0–5s | High-Density Chaos Grid | Tense, claustrophobic |
| II | **The Turn** | 150–270 | 5–9s | Pure White Grid Snap | Drop, release, clarity |
| III | **The Booking Flow** | 270–630 | 9–21s | Cool Gray & Crisp White | Precise, efficient rhythm |
| IV | **Payment & Sync** | 630–780 | 21–26s | Cool Gray & Structural Blue | High momentum, proof |
| V | **Resolve** | 780–900 | 26–30s | Pure White | Brand Outro & CTA |

---

## 2. Detailed Storyboard Beats

### Act I: The Friction (0 - 5s)
- **Visuals**: Dense, chaotic grids. Notification cards stacking up with harsh borders:
  - *"Call hold: 18 minutes"*
  - *"Reception busy tone"*
  - *"Billing Desk queue: 45m wait time"*
- **Type**: Geometric sans-serif: *"Booking a doctor should take seconds."* / beat / *"Not phone queues."*
- **Sound**: Clinical, precise clockwork ticks.

### Act II: The Turn (5 - 9s)
- **Visuals**: A radical transition into a perfect, clean structural grid.
- **Logo**: A clean LeapCreww mark reveals alongside the text: *"LeapCreww Healthcare"*.
- **Type**: Crisp reveal: *"Put patient bookings on autopilot."*
- **Sound**: Clean frequency sweep leading into the drop.

### Act III: The Booking Flow (9 - 21s)
- **Visuals**: A sleek, structural mobile phone frame slides into view.
- **WhatsApp Chat Simulation**:
  - **9.0s**: Patient: *"Hi, need to book a consultation."*
  - **11.0s**: Bot: *"Select Department: Cardiology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics"* -> User taps **Cardiology** (button clicks sharply).
  - **13.5s**: Bot: *"Select Doctor: Dr. Sharma, Dr. Verma"* -> User taps **Dr. Sharma**.
  - **16.5s**: Bot: *"Select Slot: Tomorrow 10:00 AM, Monday 2:00 PM"* -> User taps **10:00 AM**.
- **Camera**: Virtual camera smoothly pans and follows the input cards on the strict grid.
- **Sound**: Electronic arpeggios that feel technical and efficient.

### Act IV: Payment & Sync (21 - 26s)
- **Visuals**: The WhatsApp chat interface slides down as a highly structured payment overlay pulls up.
- **Payment Action**: Shows amount `₹100` and consultation details. Loader spins -> Sharp checkmark reveals: *"₹100 Paid Successfully"*.
- **Sync**: The mobile screen scales back. The camera pans to a beautiful desktop screen mock representing the hospital reception terminal, using Swiss data tables.
- **Dashboard**: A live booking line gets added automatically:
  - `Gaurav Jiju | Dr. Sharma (Cardiology) | Confirmed | Paid ₹100`
- **Sound**: Rhythmic drive holds.

### Act V: Resolve (26 - 30s)
- **Visuals**: Desktop and mobile screens slide out. Clean brand lockup emerges.
- **Tagline**: *"Better patient experience. Zero reception overhead."*
- **CTA**: *"Automate your clinic today: leapcreww.com/health"*
- **Sound**: Peaceful, structured tail fading out.

## 3. Style Guidelines (Anti-AI Slop Compliant: Playbook 2: Swiss System)
1. **No Neon Blobs**: Rely on absolute precision, grid layouts, and stark light mode environments.
2. **Typography**: Strict geometric functional font (`Inter`) and technical tabular fields in mono (`JetBrains Mono` or similar system mono).
3. **Materials**: Highly visible cell borders (`1px solid #E5E5E5`), stark pure white (`#FFFFFF`) or light cool gray (`#F8F9FA`), pitch black text (`#000000`), high-visibility blue accent (`#0055FF`). No heavy drop shadows.
